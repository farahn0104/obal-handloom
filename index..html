1. Project Overview

Product Name

Obal Handloom Management System

Product Vision

Ek comprehensive web-based management system jo Obal Handloom ke saare business operations ko digitize aur streamline karega - material management se lekar billing, payments, production tracking aur vendor management tak.

Business Context

Obal Handloom ek textile manufacturing company hai jo Gamcha, Lungi aur related products banati hai. Company apne power looms aur external weavers (baani binne wale) dono se production karati hai. Is system ka primary goal hai complete transparency, accurate tracking, aur efficient financial management ensure karna.

‚úÖ Success Metrics

  • Material shrinkage 5% ke andar control karna
  • Bill generation time 80% reduce karna
  • Payment tracking 100% accurate
  • Manual errors eliminate karna
  • Real-time production visibility

3. Material & Production Management

3.1 Material Issue to Baani

Material issue karte waqt following details capture hongi:

Required Fields:

  • Date of issue
  • Baani binne wala name (dropdown - registered profiles)
  • Material type:
    • Dhaaga (Thread): Color selection + Quantity in KG
    • Beam: Beam name/ID, Product type, Associated dhaaga quantity, Rate per piece

3.2 Product Receipt from Baani

Auto Calculations:

  • Total amount = Quantity √ó Rate per piece
  • Material consumed = Issued material - Returned material - Product weight
  • Shrinkage % = (Material consumed - Expected) / Issued √ó 100
  • Alert agar shrinkage > 5%

3.3 Shrinkage Management

Shrinkage % Status Action
< 5% Acceptable No action needed
5-7% Warning Monitor closely
> 7% Critical Alert + Investigation

3.4 Baani Profile Management

  • Bank Details: Account Number, IFSC, UPI ID
  • Material Tracking: Pending material (color-wise)
  • Production History: Complete transaction log
  • Payment History: All payments with modes

10. User Management & Authentication

10.1 Multi-Factor Authentication

üîê Two-Step Login Process:

Step 1: Username/Email + Password

  • Enter registered email/mobile
  • Enter password (strong password requirements)
  • "Remember me" option

Step 2: OTP Verification

  • OTP sent to registered mobile (SMS)
  • OTP sent to registered email
  • Enter 6-digit OTP (valid for 5 minutes)
  • Resend OTP option (after 30 seconds)

10.2 Security Features

Feature Implementation
Failed Login Attempts Max 3 attempts ‚Üí 15 min lockout
Password Requirements Min 8 chars, 1 upper, 1 lower, 1 number, 1 special
Session Timeout 30 minutes of inactivity
Password Reset Via OTP verification
Activity Logging All critical actions tracked
